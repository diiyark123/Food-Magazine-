Kaalan

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Servings:

Calories:

10min

30min

2

420 kcal

Ingredients

    For mushroom pakoda

  • 2 cup mushroom (finely chopped)
  • 1 cup cabbage (finely chopped)
  • ½ onion (finely chopped)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup maida / plain flour
  • ¼ cup cornflour
  • oil (for frying)

    • For Sauce

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • ½ onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp pepper powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 cup water
  • few curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

Recipe

  1. In a large bowl take 2 cups of mushroom, 1 cup of cabbage and ½ onion
  2. Add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp garam masala, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and ½ tsp salt.
  3. Further, add ½ cup maida and ¼ cup cornflour; Mix well forming a dough.
  4. Wet your hands with water and scoop a ball sized mushroom pakoda batter.
  5. Drop the pakoda batter in hot oil keeping the flame on medium. Stir occasionally, and fry for at least 10 minutes or until the pakoda turns crisp.
  6. In a large wok, heat 2 tbsp oil. add ½ onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute well.
  7. Keeping the flame on low add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp garam masala, ½ tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp pepper powder and ¼ tsp salt.
  8. Now add 2 tbsp tomato sauce, 2 tbsp vinegar and 2 tbsp soy sauce. Stir fry on high flame until the sauces are combined well.
  9. Further, add 1 cup cornflour slurry and mix well. (To prepare cornflour slurry, in a small bowl take 2 tbsp corn flour and 1 cup water. mix well forming a lump-free batter) Also add few curry leaves and cook until the slurry turns glossy.
  10. Now add prepared mushroom pakoda and stir well for a minute allowing the sauce to alsorb.
  11. Finally, add in 2 tbsp coriander and mix well
  12. Serve your hot kaalan with onion and coriander toppings.
