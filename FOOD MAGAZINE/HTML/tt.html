Tips & Tools

To make soft and fluffy rotis:

If you want your rotis to be soft when you bite in them, then
knead the dough with warm water. Also, make sure to rest the
dough for at least 15 minutes before cooking!












To make crispy pooris:

Simply add rava to your dough as it helps the pooris be
crispy and tasty.

While making an Indian dish, cook ingrediants in this order - Onion, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, and then spices.







To retain the green color of peas, add a pinch of sugar before you boil it.
