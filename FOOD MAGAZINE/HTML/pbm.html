Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

    For masala paste

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 5 cloves)
  • 2 pods cardamom
  • 2 onion (sliced)
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 3 tomato (sliced)
  • 5 cashew

    • For Curry

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 chilli
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ¾ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • 16 cubes paneer
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

Recipe

  1. In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbsp butter. Add 5 cloves, 2 pods cardamom and saute on low flame until it turns aromatic.
  2. Now add 2 onion, 2 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger and saute until onions shrink slightly.
  3. Further add 3 tomato, 15 cashew and saute for 2 minutes. Cover and boil for 10 minutes, or until tomato softens.
  4. Cool completely and transfer to the blender and blend to smooth paste.
  5. Pass the curry to the filter to get rid of seeds and skin.
  6. Now, in a large kadai, heat 2 tbsp oil, 2 tbsp butter, 1 bay leaf and 1 chilli; Saute until the spices turn aromatic.
  7. Now add 1 onion and saute until onions turn and golden brown.
  8. Keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ¾ tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder, ½ garam masala and 1 tsp salt. Saute until the spices turn aromatic, and make sure not to burn the spices.
  9. Add in the prepared onion tomato paste and mix well. Cook until the oil separates from the masala paste.
  10. Now add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting the consistency.
  11. Further, add in 2 tbsp cream and mix well, followed by 16 cubes of paneer.
  12. Simmer for 5 minutes, or until the paneer absorbs the flavour, add 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander and mix well.
  13. Enjoy the delicious Paneer Butter Masala with roti or naan.
