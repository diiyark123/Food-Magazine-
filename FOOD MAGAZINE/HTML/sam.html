Samosa

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Resting Time:

Servings:

10min

1hr

20min

8 samosas

Ingredients

    For dough

  • 2 cup maida / plain flour
  • ¼ tsp ajwain / carom seeds
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ cup oil
  • ½ cup water

    • For stuffing

  • 2 tsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin / jeera
  • ½ tsp coriander seeds (crushed)
  • ½ tsp fennel
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 chilli (finely chopped)
  • ½ cup peas / matar
  • ½ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp cumin powder / jeera powder
  • ½ tsp aamchur / dry mango powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ¼ tsp pepper (crushed)
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 4 potato
  • 5 cashew
  • 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

    • Other ingrediants

  • Water(for sealing
  • Oil(for deep frying)

Recipe

  1. Pinch a ball sized dough and grease with oil. Roll the dough into oval shape. Now, cut it horizontally, diving into 2 equal portions using a knife, grease with water and make cone.
  2. Stuff 2 tbsp of prepared samosa masala into the cone. Now grease little water on the edges and close and seal tightly by pressing firmly.
  3. eep-fry the samosa on a low flame. alternatively bake at 180 degree celcius for 40 minutes. stir occasionally, frying the samosa on low flame for atleast 15 minutes. once the aloo samosa turns golden and crisp, drain off over kitchen paper.
  4. Deep-fry the samosa on a low flame. alternatively bake at 180 degree celcius for 40 minutes. Stir occasionally, frying the samosa on low flame for atleast 15 minutes.
  5. Once the aloo samosa turns golden and crisp, drain off over kitchen paper
